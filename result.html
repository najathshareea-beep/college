About Our Institution

Academic Excellence

Founded by the visionary Sheikhuna K.T. Manu Musliyar, Darunnajath Shareeath & Arts College stands as an A+ Graded Junior College under the esteemed Pattikkad Jamia Nooriyya Arabiyya. We offer a unique integrated educational pathway that begins after 7th standard and continues through higher education, providing both traditional Islamic education under Samastha and modern academic programs. Our institution seamlessly bridges school education with higher religious studies, creating a comprehensive learning ecosystem that nurtures both academic excellence and spiritual growth.

Our Educational Pathway

  • After 7th Standard: Regular school education with excellent hostel facilities
  • After SSLC: Direct admission to six-year comprehensive Najawi degree program
  • Traditional Islamic education curriculum under Samastha guidance
  • Complete SSLC, +2, Degree, and PG programs in secular education
  • Specialized Dawra programs for Hafiz students

Infrastructure & Facilities

  • WiFi-connected computer lab and digital library
  • Spacious library with extensive reference hall
  • Smart classrooms with modern teaching aids
  • Extensive playground and sports facilities
  • Comfortable hostel accommodation with modern amenities

Special Features

  • Multi-language proficiency in Arabic, Urdu, English & Malayalam
  • Special training in public speaking and khutba delivery
  • Creative writing and personality development programs
  • Regular art, literature competitions and cultural events
  • Educational tours and exposure visits
  • Imam training and leadership development
