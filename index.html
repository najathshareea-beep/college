About Our College

Darunnajath Shareeath & Arts College is an institution where faith and knowledge grow together. As an A+ accredited junior college under Jamia Nooriyya Arabiyya, Pattikkad, Darunnajath operates under the strict guidance of Samastha Kerala Jam'iyyathul Ulama.

Founded by the renowned scholar and educational activist K.T. Manu Musliyar, this institution provides the unique facility of integrating Islamic and secular education from the seventh grade onwards in the same campus.

Here, students receive simultaneously: High-quality general education (SSLC, Plus Two, Degree, PG), authentic six-year Najavi degree, and Daura for Hafizs. Excellent teaching staff, modern hostel facilities, and a peaceful learning environment are the specialties of this campus.
